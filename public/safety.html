Safety Center

Your safety is our top priority. We have built several features into Rovefix to ensure a secure experience for everyone.

Verification PIN

Always wait for the technician to ask for your unique 4-digit PIN before they start work. This confirms they are the person assigned to your job.

ID Verification

Every technician on our platform must upload an government-issued ID (Aadhaar) which is manually reviewed by our admin team.

Emergency Priority

If you have an urgent repair, flag it as an Emergency. These jobs are prioritized in our system and sent to the nearest experts immediately.

Reporting System

If you experience any unprofessional behavior, use the "Report User" button in the app. Our team investigates every report within 24 hours.
